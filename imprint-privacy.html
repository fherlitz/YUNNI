1. Introduction

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains what information we process when you visit yunnimusic.com, why we process it, and how you can exercise your rights.

2. Data Processing

We process personal data only to the extent necessary to operate this website and respond to enquiries:

  • Server log files: When you access the site, GitHub Pages and Cloudflare automatically record technical information (IP address, date/time, requested page, referrer and user-agent). These logs are used exclusively for operational integrity and security and are deleted automatically after a short retention period. The logs are not combined with other data.
  • Direct communication: Personal data that you submit when contacting us by e-mail (e.g. your address, the contents of your message) will be processed only for the purpose of handling your enquiry.

3. Cookies & Third-party Content

yunnimusic.com does not set first-party cookies. The site embeds YouTube videos in "privacy-enhanced" mode, which means Google/YouTube will set cookies only after you click Play. These cookies are beyond our control. You can block them via your browser settings without affecting the general usability of the website.

4. Third-Party Links

This website includes links to our social media platforms. Clicking on those links may allow third parties to collect or share data about you. We do not control these third-party websites and are not responsible for their privacy statements.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website includes content and links to third-party services:

  • YouTube: We use YouTube's privacy-enhanced mode for embedded videos, which doesn't set cookies until you click play. When you interact with YouTube content, YouTube's privacy policy applies.
  • Spotify: Links to our music on Spotify may set cookies when you visit their platform. Spotify's privacy policy applies to any data collected.
  • Bandcamp: Links to our music on Bandcamp may set cookies when you visit their platform. Bandcamp's privacy policy applies to any data collected.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at juaneparga@gmail.com.
